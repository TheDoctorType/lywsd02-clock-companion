Add mouse-wheel zoom to the trends window

Scroll the wheel over a chart to zoom the time axis in/out, centred on the cursor
(AxisX.ScaleView), from the full dataset down to a ~3-sample window. Both stacked
charts zoom together so the clock and Aranet stay time-aligned, each clamped to
its own data range; a scrollbar appears for panning when zoomed, and scrolling
fully out resets to the full view. Charts take focus on hover so they receive the
wheel. Changing the Range filter resets the zoom.
